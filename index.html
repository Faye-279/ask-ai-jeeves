So… Who Was Jeeves, Anyway?

Jeeves was the polite butler behind Ask Jeeves, a search engine launched in 1997 that let you ask full questions like “Why is the sky blue?” instead of just typing keywords. It stood out by trying to mimic human understanding—well before AI assistants made that normal.

At his peak in the early 2000s, Jeeves was everywhere—then Google took over, keyword search won out, and Jeeves was quietly “retired” in 2006. Jeeves over the years ↗
